Our Story

Will's Pub is basically a neighborhood bar that was colonized by the music community as soon as it opened in 1995. The now-institutionalized culture and ethos has turned it into one of the Sunshine State's most established live music venues and an anchor of the city's creative district.

Our Event Center

With an occupancy of 200, this church of rock has hosted acts as diverse as at the Drive-in, The Decemberists, Maserati, The Queers, Lucero, Drivin 'N' Cryin', Astronautalis. As one of Central Florida's premier indie venues, we're always looking for fresh talent and would love to be a regular, reliable and established stage for your touring roster.
